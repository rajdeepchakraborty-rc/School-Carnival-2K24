About

School Carnival 2K24 is a celebration of creativity, talent, and camaraderie, offering an unforgettable experience filled with exciting games, engaging activities, and opportunities for students to shine. It's a vibrant platform where fun meets learning, and imaginations come alive. Join us to create lasting memories, foster friendships, and explore endless possibilities.
